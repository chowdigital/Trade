A MEMBERS’ CLUB FOR THE HOSPITALITY COMMUNITY

TRADE is a social space with a focus on quality food and drink at industry-friendly prices, where like-minded hospitality professionals can unwind with peers outside working hours.

A hotbed for networking, idea-sharing, training and inspiring the next generation of talented individuals.

UPCOMING EVENTS

TRADE will run a varied programme of member events including wine and spirits tastings, bar takeovers with guest bartenders, ‘An Evening With…’ sessions with industry legends, plus regular events, tastings and roundtables, organised by TRADE, or in conjunction with hospitality industry partners including CODE and Imbibe. Members’ can book places online. Full schedule available here soon.
